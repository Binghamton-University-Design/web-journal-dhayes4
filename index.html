Project 1

Running Out of Time

The goal here was to show the progression of the state of the Earth over time and how we are in a downwards spiral in terms of things like global warming and climate change.

This serves as a call for help involving the state of the environment here on Earth. Over time, we as humans have contributed to damaging the Earth in many different ways. If we continue at the rate we are going at currently, we will completely destroy our planet.
